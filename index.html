    Produck is a project management tool that helps you organize and plan tasks, monitor progress, and integrate with Google Calendar and Google Drive for easy access to your docs and files. You can also visualize tasks in Kanban boards and sprints, and integrate with Zapier. The software is used by freelancers, project managers, small, medium, and large companies.
